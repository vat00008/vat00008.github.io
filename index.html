Description:

Burn After Reading follows 2 ordinary people accidentally getting ahold of a secret government document, and the chaos that ensues when they try to benefit from the situation.
The comedy comes from the satirical depiction of how the government operates, and the movie makes a a point
of reminding us that official agencies are composed of normal, flawed humans.
The bureaucracy through which the characters navigate is also somewhat reminiscent of Franz Kafka's works.
